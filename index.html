Time to Move

Enjoy exercise on busy days

Me-Mover allows you to enjoy exercise while you go about your day. You get a complete workout with an effectiveness that out-performs running, jogging and cycling, while avoiding the long-term strain and damage that often comes with traditional exercises.

Minimal Impact on Joints

The G-force impact is lower on the Me-Mover than walking

Complete Work-Out

Train 6 of 8 major muscle groups, while building balance, strength and stamina

Stand Up Straight

The ergonomically correct, upright position improves your posture and protects your back and neck health
