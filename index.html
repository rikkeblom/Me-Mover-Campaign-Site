Time to Move

Enjoy exercise on busy days

Me-Mover allows you to enjoy exercise while you go about your day. You get a complete workout with an effectiveness that out-performs running, jogging and cycling, while avoiding the long-term strain and damage that often comes with traditional exercises.

Minimal Impact on Joints

The G-force impact is lower on the Me-Mover than walking

Complete Work-Out

Train 6 of 8 major muscle groups, while building balance, strength and stamina

Stand Up Straight

The ergonomically correct, upright position improves your posture and protects your back and neck health

A Unique Training Experience

Me-Mover is a Danish engineered exercise vehicle designed for the busy professional. As the Me-Mover is the first product of its kind on the Danish market, we offer free test rides, so you can experience the movement before committing.

Me-Mover takes up less space than a cargo-bike and can go as fast as a regular bike. With it's 3-wheeled design Me-Mover offers stability and excellent body posture. Each Me-Mover is made to Scandinavian design standards, from high quality T6 tempered aluminium for maximum strength and durability.
